About Me

I'm a passionate game design student at the University of Arizona with a love for both visual creativity and technical problem-solving. I specialize in 2D pixel art and enjoy bringing characters and worlds to life with tools like Aseprite. On the technical side, I’m drawn to backend development in Unity, especially scripting complex systems and gameplay mechanics in 3D environments.

What drives me most is coming up with fresh game ideas! Whether it’s a unique gameplay loop, an inventive world, or a new take on a genre. I love working on collaborative teams, blending art, code, and design to create something truly memorable.
